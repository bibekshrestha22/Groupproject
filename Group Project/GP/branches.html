About Urban Hotel Kyoto Gojo Premium

Our hotel had its grand opening on March 16, 2020. It is near the Aburanokoji-Gojo crossing, where centuries-old history and culture remain strongly in evidence. Stay with us and unwind your body and mind. Our restaurant has an open ambience. Urban Hotel Kyoto Nijo Premium

About Urban Hotel Kyoto NIjo Premium

Opened in 2015, Urban Hotel Kyoto Nijo Premium is conveniently located just a 5-mintue walk from JR and metro Nijo stations. Guests can unwind in a large public bath after spending a day exploring the city. Free WiFi can be used for free throughout the property. Clean and modern rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV, a fridge and a desk. The private bathroom features a shower and a hairdryer. The Premium Urban Hotel offers free luggage storage services at the 24-hour front desk. Drinks vending machines are available on site. The hotel is a 15-minute walk to Nijo Castle, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. JR Kyoto Station is just a 6-minute train ride away, and Fushimi Inari Shrine can be accessed within a 15-minute train ride. Nijo Premium Urban Hotel Kyoto also offers easy bus access to Shijo Kawaramachi and Arashiyama as there is a bus stop right in front of the hotel.Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 8.5 for a two-person trip.
